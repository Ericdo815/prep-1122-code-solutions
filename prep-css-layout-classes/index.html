Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

A 1981 American action film directed by Steven Spielberg that it based on a true story. Harrison Ford portrays Indiana Jones, an archaeologist vying with Nazi forces to recover the long-lost Ark of the Covenant

Temple of Doom

A 1984 action-adventure film directed by Steven Spielberg that is a sequel to the original Raiders of the Lost Ark. Indiana Jones sets out to find a mystical stone and save a group of children from a cult known for slavery and human sacrifice.

The Last Crusade

A 1989 Steven Speilberg action-adventure film that is the third installment of the Indiana Jones series. Indiana Jones sets out to find his father, who has been kidnapped and held hostage by the Nazis
